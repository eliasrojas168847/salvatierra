Our Work

ProfitMania works as a Consultant / Advisor or as an Asset / Project manager, depending on our client needs.

We also create and develop our own projects and initiatives currently being promoted in different parts of the world. We are committed to delivering cost-effective and profitable results to our clients, while acting responsibly with our community.

An example is "Amazon Village" a multi-family rural development, combining housing with agricultural and fish production for sustainable household revenue generation.

It provides a solution to reduce migration from rural areas into larger cities, while offering the necessary training, technology, production and commercial tools to ensure profitable/sustainable operations.
